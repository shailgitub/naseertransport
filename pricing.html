Pricing FAQs

Answers to common questions about our costs

Why does my quote vary from the estimated price?

Final pricing depends on several factors we can't account for in estimates: exact weight after packing, parking accessibility at both locations, need for special equipment (like cranes), and whether you require additional services like deep cleaning. We always provide a binding quote after physical inspection.

How can I reduce my moving costs?

You can save up to 30% by: 1) Self-packing non-fragile items, 2) Choosing weekday vs weekend moves, 3) Being flexible with dates for off-peak discounts, 4) Opting for shared-load transport when possible, and 5) Booking at least 3 weeks in advance for early-bird rates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment options: UPI (PhonePe, GPay), credit/debit cards (2% convenience fee), net banking, and cash (up to ₹2 lakhs as per RBI guidelines). Corporate clients can avail 15-30 day credit terms after verification.
